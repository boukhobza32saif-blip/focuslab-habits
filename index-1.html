📋 How to Use FocusLab

Simple, powerful habit tracking for real discipline

1

Set your habits

Click any habit name to rename it. Click + Add to add new habits.

2

Click to mark done

Click any day cell — it fills green with ✓. Click again to undo. Each click = +1 point.

3

Keep your streak 🔥

Complete ALL habits every day to grow your streak. Never miss 2 days in a row.

4

Rate mental state

Go to Charts and rate Mood & Motivation 1-10 each day.

5

Switch months

Click any month tab at the top. Data saves automatically in your browser.

6

View yearly stats

Go to Stats tab to see your progress across all 12 months.

💡 Tips for 85%+ discipline

  • Check habits at the same time every day
  • Never miss 2 days in a row
  • Start with 3-5 habits, grow slowly
  • Your streak number is your most powerful motivator
